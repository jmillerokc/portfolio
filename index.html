01 / Diagnosis

Speed-to-lead as the growth lever.

CRM timestamp analysis surfaced the real lever: how quickly sales reached out. Engagement and downstream outcomes both decayed sharply with delay.

Key Insight
A simple sales enablement plan, paired with a light tech implementation for lead routing, made a measurable difference. Speed-to-lead drove higher engagement and more closed deals by aligning outreach with peak buyer intent.
02 / Compounding Issue

Sequence engagement was suppressing pipeline impact.

Initial assumptions blamed list quality or creative fatigue. Segmentation pointed somewhere else.

Channel
Diagnosis

One-off campaigns met or exceeded benchmark. Automated sequence emails consistently underperformed on both open rate and CTR. The issue was not channel-wide, it was isolated to nurture sequences.

  • Sequences relied on generic, non-contextual messaging.
  • No differentiation by industry or role.
  • Engagement declined as messaging became less relevant.
03 / Intervention

Restructured sequences with contextual messaging.

Each touchpoint was aligned to industry-specific challenges and role-specific decision drivers.

Takeaway
The fix was not more volume or more channels, it was more relevance. Treating each recipient as a person with a specific industry context and role made every other GTM investment work harder.
